My interest - Basketball

Basketball is a simple game, where the main goal is to throw the ball in the basket more often than the opposition. You can shoot from anywhere, but shots inside the 3-point line the large semi-circle on each side of the court are worth 2, and anything outside is worth 3.

Position that i play

Point guard, it's essential to have good ball-handling skills, be able to make accurate passes, and have good court vision.
